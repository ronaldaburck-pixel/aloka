About Aloka

Aloka by Rutushree brings you handcrafted soy candles that embody calm luxury and mindful living. Each candle is carefully poured by hand using 100% pure soy wax, creating a clean, long-lasting burn that fills your space with warmth and tranquility.

Our commitment to eco-friendly practices means every candle is made with sustainable materials, ensuring you can enjoy your favorite scents while caring for the planet. Whether for your home or as a thoughtful gift, Aloka candles bring a touch of elegance and serenity to any moment.
